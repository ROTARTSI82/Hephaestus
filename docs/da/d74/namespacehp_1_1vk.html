◆ max_frames_in_flight

const int hp::vk::max_frames_in_flight = 2

Maximum number of frames the Vulkan renderer would attempt to process concurrently.

Note
Do not set too low or too high; Too low and the renderer would waste time sitting idly, Too high and it gets bottle-necked.
